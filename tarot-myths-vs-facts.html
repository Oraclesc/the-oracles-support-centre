Tarot Myths vs Facts

Tarot has been surrounded by myths for generations. Some are harmless traditions, while others discourage people from learning altogether. Let's look at some of the most common beliefs and compare them with the facts.

🎁

Myth: Your First Deck Must Be a Gift

Fact: You can absolutely buy your own tarot deck.

Being gifted a deck is a tradition in some communities, but it has never been a universal rule.

Many experienced readers purchased their own first deck.

🔮

Myth: You Need Psychic Powers

Fact: Tarot can be learned by anyone.

Observation, practice and reflection are far more important than claiming special abilities.

Most readers improve through experience rather than supernatural gifts.

👤

Myth: You Cannot Read Your Own Cards

Fact: Many people regularly read for themselves.

The main challenge is remaining objective.

Keeping a tarot journal can help reduce personal bias.

☠

Myth: Tarot Predicts Death

Fact: Tarot cards should not be treated as a way to predict death or certain future events.

Cards such as Death usually represent endings, change, transformation or new beginnings rather than physical death.
